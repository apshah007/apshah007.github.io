#100hats Project

This volunteer service project spans from 2018 to present.

This project was started by Amy Shah to provide handmade winter clothing items to vulnerable populations in Louisville, including people affected by poverty and drug addiction and children at the Home of the Innocents. The program provides 100+ handmade winter clothing items per year. In total, the program has donated almost 300 hats to local charities for distribution.

This project was awarded a grant by the Awesome Foundation in 2019. The project was also given donations and support by the helpful volunteers at LVL1 Hackerspace, a makerspace in Louisville, KY. In 2020, the #100hats Project participated in the Presidents Volunteer Service Award Program at the University of Louisville.
