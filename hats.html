#100hats Project

2018

This hat project spans from 2018 to present. This program provides handmade crocheted/knitted winter hats and scarves to people in need. These handmade winter clothing items will be distributed to vulnerable populations in Louisville, people affected by drug addiction, and children in need at the Home of the Innocents. The program plans to provide 100+ handmade winter clothing items to individuals. What is the nature of the service? Which beneficiaries does it serve? What goals does it aim to achieve? We need more handmade hats and scarves to donate. Handmade items show that we care. Many people who have insecure housing don't have places to store winter clothing and may not have hats and scarves for the coming winter season. We aim to make over 100 hats/scarves for people in need for the winter this year. We have already started making the clothing items and donating them to people in need. We have donated to Centerstone West Broadway, Centerstone Addiction Recovery Center, and to the Home of the Innocents in the past.
